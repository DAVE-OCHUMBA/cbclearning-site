The Problem

Kenyan schools are
drowning in paper.

The CBC curriculum is transformative — but the tools to run it haven't caught up. Schools are managing everything manually.

📋
CBC Reporting Overload
Teachers spend up to 40% of their time on CBC competency paperwork — recording EE, ME, AE, BE levels for each student manually across 7 competencies every term.
40% of teacher time lost to admin
🏦
Bank Queue Chaos
Parents queue 2+ hours at bank branches to pay school fees in cash. Schools collect only 83% of fees — the rest is lost to the friction of cash handling and manual reconciliation.
Only 83% collection rate on cash
📶
No Reliable Internet
Upcountry schools have unreliable connectivity. Existing EdTech solutions fail when the internet drops — which is precisely when upcountry teachers need them most.
72% of schools: unreliable internet

Three problems solved.

CBC Learning Ecosystem is the first school management platform built end-to-end for Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, integrated with M-Pesa, and designed to work without internet.
